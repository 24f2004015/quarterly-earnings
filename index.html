Financial Metrics

Net Interest Margin (NIM):

\( \text{NIM} = \frac{\text{Interest Income} - \text{Interest Expense}}{\text{Average Earning Assets}} \)

Example: If Interest Income = 120, Interest Expense = 30, Average Earning Assets = 2,000 ⇒ \(\text{NIM} = \frac{120-30}{2000}=4.5\%\)
