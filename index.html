Financial Formula

Net Interest Margin (NIM):

\( \text{NIM} = \dfrac{\text{Interest Income} - \text{Interest Expense}}{\text{Average Earning Assets}} \)

Example: \( \dfrac{120 - 30}{2000} = 4.5\% \)
